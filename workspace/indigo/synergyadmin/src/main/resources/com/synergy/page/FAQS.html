Synergy Chat FAQ | Cloud Computing | Internet Video Chat, Mail & Conference






1-What is SynergyChat?
Synergychat is a simple application that attaches to any existing site on the web. It invokes functions that allow video chat, video message and video conference from that site. Purchasers of synegychat service should have webcams, microphone/speakers or headsets to use all of its features. However, visitors can use just text and can see you if they do not have mics and cams.

2- How does SynergyChat work?
Synergychat uses technology called cloud computing. Cloud computing is software that runs on the web. There are no downloads to your computer and applications can be accessed from any computer with a browser.

3- Does my browser need to accept cookies to use SynergyChat?
No. We are a subscription service we do not advertise, sell or track your information.


SYNERGYCHAT ONLINE MEETINGS AND VIDEO CHAT

1-How do I join an online meeting?
Meetings with video, audio and text can be accessed two ways. Directly through the site via a guest/subscriber for 2 people or by email for conferences with up to 9 people.

2-Is SynergyChat account needed to attend a meeting?
Only 1 connection is needed to host a meeting with up to 9 people.

3- Are there any system requirements needed to be able to attend a meeting?
You must have a browser, a valid email and flash installed on that browser. Flash can be downloaded free at www.adobe.com. Most browsers come with this software installed on it. In fact, most sites on the web are flash enabled sites.

4- Do I need the newest version of Flash to run SynergyChat with my computer?
If it installs on your browser it will work. Adobe does update their flash program but older versions are supported on our software.

5- How can I change my password on my SynergyChat account?
A password can be changed inside the main account and individual access users can be also added and deleted by the administrator of the site.

6- How do I buy more connections?
Once installed connections can be added by going to synergychat.com. Click on payment and activation tab to buy more connections,

7- How do I schedule a meeting?
Meetings are schedule via email. All participants including the meeting organizer access links in their email. While in while in a meeting email invites can be forwarded from your email or mobile device.
